How does it work?

In this our 24x7 society why wait days/weeks for an appointment. At a time convenient to you follow the 6 simple steps below at your convenience. Most of the consultations can be done by email, skype or phone allowing you to look after your health without having to rearrange your life.

Take the following simple steps

Step 1
Contact us via the online form.
Step 2
We will contact you to recommend and discuss the appropriate package.
Step 3
A blood draw is required. There are two options a) You can use a clinic which we have an established relationship with or b) we can send you a blood form and pack so your GP or local clinic can take your blood. This is then returned to us.
Step 4
We analyse your results based on the information provided in your registration form and pre-consultation.
Step 5
Our CuroSeven report and recommendations will be made available to you via our secure website. You can track changes in your blood markers over time based on recommended interventions.
Step 6
Choose your follow-up appointment or consult at your convenience.You make the choices and we make them work for you.
