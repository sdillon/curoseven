Privacy

Curoseven is committed to protecting your privacy when you use this website. We strive to protect the personal information under our control and will use appropriate technical and organisational measures to help maintain the security and integrity of that data. You can be assured that your personal information will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement.By using this website, you unconditionally agree to be bound by this privacy statement.

You may be asked for information about yourself, such as name, e-mail address, telephone number and personal medical details. You are under no obligation to supply this information but without it we may not be able to supply you with certain services.
